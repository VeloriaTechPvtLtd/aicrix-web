AICRIX runs five specialized machine learning models in parallel for every match. Each model focuses on a different aspect: win probability, toss impact, player form, team strength, and venue behavior.

We ingest decades of historical match data, live weather feeds, and pitch reports. The models analyze patterns, run Monte Carlo simulations, and produce confidence-weighted predictions seconds before each match.

The result is a single, coherent output: win probabilities for both teams, key player impact scores, toss advantage, and venue intelligence — all backed by 89%+ historical accuracy.
