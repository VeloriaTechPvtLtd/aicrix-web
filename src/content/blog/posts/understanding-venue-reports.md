Every cricket ground has its own character. MCG behaves differently from Wankhede; Lord's is not Chinnaswamy. AICRIX captures these differences in our Venue Intelligence module.

We track batting-first win rates, average first-innings totals, dew and weather impact, and outfield pace. This data feeds into toss impact analysis and run-scoring expectations.

Venue reports are shown for every match in the app. Use them to understand why the toss might matter more at certain grounds, or when to expect high-scoring encounters.
