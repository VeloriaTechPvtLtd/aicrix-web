## Tone & Voice

- **Overall:** Professional, confident, data-led, and cricket-passionate. Clear and concise; avoid fluff or hype without substance.
- **Style:** Use short sentences and concrete numbers (percentages, data points, years) where relevant. Slight tech/analytics feel without jargon overload.
- **Do:** Emphasize accuracy, transparency, real-time, ML/AI, historical depth, zero editorial bias, "actionable insights," "data-driven."
- **Avoid:** Overpromising, gambling/betting encouragement (we explicitly don’t endorse betting), vague claims, childish or meme-heavy tone.
- **Disclaimer tone:** Predictions are for informational and entertainment only; not a guarantee; we do not facilitate or endorse betting or gambling.

## Visual & Theme (Design System)

- **Primary palette (dark theme):**
  - **Background:** `#050810` (deep navy-black), `#080d1a`, `#0d1426` for layers.
  - **Surface / cards:** `#101828`, `#162035`.
  - **Primary accent (brand green):** `#00e5a0` (mint/teal) — CTAs, highlights, success, key metrics.
  - **Secondary accents:** Cyan `#0af`, purple `#7c6aff` for variety (e.g. step 2, step 3, badges).
  - **Destructive / negative:** `#ff4d6d` (red) — losses, down, warnings.
  - **Text:** Primary `#e8edf5`; muted/secondary `#7a8aaa`.
  - **Borders:** `rgba(255,255,255,0.07)`.
- **Typography:**  
  - **Font:** Inter for UI and headings (fallback: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif).  
  - **Headings:** Bold (700–800), tight letter-spacing (-0.5px to -1.5px), clear hierarchy.  
  - **Body:** ~1.05–1.1rem, line-height ~1.6; muted color for secondary text.
- **Components:**  
  - Buttons: Primary = mint background (`#00e5a0`), black text, rounded (8px), hover lift + soft glow.  
  - Outline buttons: transparent, mint border, mint text.  
  - Cards: dark surface, thin light border, rounded (12–16px).  
  - Badges/tags: small caps, letter-spacing, mint or purple tint for accents.  
  - Section tags: small uppercase label above headings, often with a small accent line (e.g. mint).
- **Atmosphere:** Dark, techy, premium. Subtle noise/grain overlay optional. Soft glows (mint/cyan/purple) for hero or key sections; no bright neon. Accessible contrast.
